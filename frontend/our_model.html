LRv1

  • Developer: Niramaya Team
  • Model Type: Logistic Regression
  • Accuracy: 0.91 (identical to LRv2)
  • F1 Score: 0.89 (identical to LRv2)
  • Recall: 0.91 (identical to LRv2)
  • Precision: 0.89 (identical to LRv2)

  • Outputs: Predicted disease and description
  • Description: LRv1 analyzes your symptoms using a logistic regression model to predict probable diseases, providing early awareness with detailed descriptions.

LRv2

  • Developer: Niramaya Team
  • Model Type: Enhanced Logistic Regression
  • Accuracy: 0.91 (identical to LRv1)
  • F1 Score: 0.89 (identical to LRv1)
  • Recall: 0.91 (identical to LRv1)
  • Precision: 0.89 (identical to LRv1)
  • Cross-Validation: 0.89 (identical to LRv1)
  • Dataset: Kaggle’s Symptoms to Disease Dataset
  • Outputs: Predicted disease, description, and precautions
  • Description: Building on LRv1, LRv2 not only predicts diseases but also offers tailored precautions, empowering you with comprehensive health guidance.
