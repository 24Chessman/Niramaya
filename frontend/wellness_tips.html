Spring

  • Eat: Fresh greens, berries, and lean proteins to boost energy.
  • Avoid: Heavy, oily foods that can cause sluggishness.
  • Stay Active: Enjoy outdoor hikes or cycling in the crisp air.

Summer

  • Eat: Hydrating fruits like watermelon and light salads.
  • Avoid: Excessive caffeine and sugary drinks that dehydrate.
  • Stay Active: Swim or jog early to beat the heat.

Autumn

  • Eat: Root vegetables, apples, and warm soups for comfort.
  • Avoid: Processed snacks and excess sugar.
  • Stay Active: Try yoga or pilates as the days cool down.

Winter

  • Eat: Hearty stews, citrus fruits, and whole grains for warmth.
  • Avoid: High-fat comfort foods and excessive alcohol.
  • Stay Active: Indoor workouts or winter sports like skiing.
